This app works with a third party app that is a social media for athletes. This third party app is called Strava. Our app uses your miles tracked on Strava to calculate your part wear on your bike!

This screen is the hub of all your bike's part wear. The screen displays the parts sorted from most worn to least. You can tap the (i) logo to view your part information!

This pop up has all of the necessary information for viewing and updating your part information!

You can use this page to update information, like your model, for your chains and cassettes!

Here is the place to change the parts that are actually on your bike! You can remove things like suspension, sealant, and a dropper seatpost from the app!
