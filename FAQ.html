Frequently Asked Questions

Q:

I just bought a used bike, how can I track that bike accurately?

A:

The App has a settings page where you can update your used parts. This page is called "Bike Register" in the bike settings page in the menu. This page updates your part wear based off of percentage given by you. We recommend taking the bike to a bike shop to have then estimate percentages for most of your parts.

Q:

Can I use this app without a shop?

A:

Yes, you can buy a personal account plan on our website or use a free limited account. We highly recommend using a participating shop's code because it greatly enhances your experience.

Q:

I signed up for a personal account and lost my code before entering it in the app. How can I get the code?

A:

You can reach out to us via email and send your information and we'll find it for you.

Q:

What if I do not have all of the parts on my bike, like front fork, rear shock, sealant, or a dropper seatpost?

A:

We have a settings page for that. You can add or remove parts from your bike profile according to your bike type.
